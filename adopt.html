ADOPTION PACK DELIVERY INFORMATION

We will process and send your order within three working days, and would normally expect it to arrive within seven working days to addresses within the UK. All UK orders are sent via Royal Mail second class delivery. Please allow 28 days for delivery on overseas orders. Please note – we cannot hold orders for delivery on a specific date.

Orders do not need to be signed for, but come in a box which won’t fit through the letterbox so you will need to be available to accept the delivery. A card will be left if you’re unavailable.

WHY ADOPT AN ANIMAL WITH THE BORN FREE FOUNDATION?

Your cash can have an immediate impact on the lives of animals across the world. Make every penny count.

More about Born Free Foundation and our mission here.

Any extra funds go towards our projects to protect the species and its habitat. The adoption keeps you personally in touch with your adopted animal and, believe me, makes a real difference.”
