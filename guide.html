Animals Guide

We are the home of dozens of different species

We are the home of dozens of different species! All of our animals live in natural-themed exhibits and we’re passionate about conserving their future and protecting them from extinction.

Here you can learn more about your favourite mammals, birds, reptiles and amphibians. Please note that species are listed in alphabetical order according to the first letter.

Please note that following the passing of our elderly elephant Duchess in July 2019, the zoo does not house any elephants.
